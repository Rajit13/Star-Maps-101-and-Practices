## ✨ Overview

**Star Charts 101 and Practices** is a structured notebook designed for students preparing for competitions like the *International Olympiad on Astronomy and Astrophysics (IOAA)*.

It combines:
- rigorous star chart interpretation  
- practical observational techniques  
- Olympiad-style reasoning  

This material is actively used in training students for the  
👉 [Bangladesh Olympiad on Astronomy and Astrophysics](https://bdoaa.org)

---

## 🚀 What you'll learn

- 🧭 How to read and interpret star maps efficiently  
- 🌠 Identifying constellations and sky regions  
- 🔭 Foundations of observational astronomy  
- 📊 Olympiad-level problem-solving strategies  
- 🧮 Geometric reasoning behind celestial motion *(implicit, not formula-heavy)*  

---

## 📘 Structure of the guide

The notebook is designed to progress from **intuition → structure → application**:

1. Basics of star charts  
2. Sky orientation and motion  
3. Practical observation techniques  
4. Problem-solving exercises  
5. (In progress) Telescopic observations  
6. (Planned) Celestial coordinate systems

This project started in **2016** after my Olympiad experience.
